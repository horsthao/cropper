About butterfly cropping

At UCL we are working on automatic tools which can be useful for ecologists.

Butterflies are an interesting species. Knowing things about butterflies, for example which species are currently living where, can help scientists in many different ways. For example, butterflies can tell us about climate change, evolution and biodiversity.

It can be difficult and time-consuming for experts to assess butterfly populations. We are working on tools to help non-experts to help biodiversity studies, and to enable automatic systems to help provide data to scientists.
